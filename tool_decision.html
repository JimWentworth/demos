Rule of Thumb

For exploration and low-risk drafting: ChatGPT, Gemini, and Copilot are all appropriate under the campus agreement.

For internal, consequential, or sensitive work: start with Illinois Chat. Use commercial tools only when you are sure the data is permitted and the risk is low.
