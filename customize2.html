Choosing the Right Level of Customization

Prompt-Level

Is this a one-off or exploratory task?

Keep it simple. Use a situational prompt when the task won’t repeat or you’re exploring ideas without long-term structure.

Persistent

Is this something you do all the time?

Add stable preferences to Custom Instructions when repetition is high—formatting, tone, reasoning steps, or accessibility needs.

Structural

Does the task require documents or formal rules?

Use RAG, projects, or agents when the AI must reference specific files, policies, or knowledge bases reliably.

Structural

Will other people depend on the results?

Build a structured tool only when consistency and shared use matter—clarified scope, curated sources, predictable behaviors.

Start Low

Can you solve this with the simplest method?

Always begin with prompt-level customization. Move up to persistent or structural methods only when lighter tooling fails.
