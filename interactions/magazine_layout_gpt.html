Understanding AI Tools

What Are Custom GPTs?

Custom GPTs are specialized versions of ChatGPT that have been tailored for specific tasks, audiences, or educational purposes. Think of them as expert assistants, each trained to excel in a particular domain or teaching approach.

Unlike the general ChatGPT interface, these custom GPTs come pre-configured with specialized instructions, knowledge, and conversational styles that make them ideal for specific educational contexts. They guide users through structured learning experiences, provide scaffolded support, and maintain consistent pedagogical approaches.

Why Use CITL's Custom GPTs?

Purpose-Built: Each GPT is designed with educational best practices and specific learning outcomes in mind.

Consistent Experience: Students and educators get reliable, predictable interactions aligned with teaching goals.

No Prompt Engineering Required: Users can jump right in without needing to craft complex prompts.

Pedagogically Sound: Built by educators, for educators, with student learning at the center.
