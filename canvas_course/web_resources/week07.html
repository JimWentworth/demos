Week 7: Case 2 (Your Second Path) — New lens, same data

Learning Goals

  • Apply a different ML approach to familiar data
  • Understand how different methods reveal different patterns
  • Compare and contrast your first and second analytical approaches
  • Build breadth in your ML toolkit

Activities for the Week

  • Select your Case 2 path (must differ from Case 1)
  • Read: Theory and methodology for your new approach
  • Watch: Case 2 method-specific tutorials
  • Revisit the shared case file data with your new lens
  • Discussion: What do you notice that you didn't see before?

Deliverable for Grading

Assignment: Case 2 Initial Approach Document

Submit a document that:

  • Describes your selected Case 2 ML approach and rationale
  • Compares this method with your Case 1 approach
  • Identifies what new questions this method might answer
  • Presents initial data preprocessing plans specific to this method
  • Outlines expected challenges and mitigation strategies

Due: End of Week 7

Points: 30
