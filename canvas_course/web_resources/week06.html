Week 6: Cross-Case Checkpoint 1 — Sharing what you've seen

Learning Goals

  • Compare and contrast different ML approaches across case studies
  • Learn from peers who took different paths through the material
  • Identify common challenges and solutions across different methods
  • Develop presentation skills for technical material

Activities for the Week

  • Present your Case 1 findings to a small group
  • Review and provide feedback on 2-3 peer case files
  • Participate in cross-case discussion forum
  • Attend "Lessons Learned" synchronous session
  • Reflect on insights gained from alternative approaches

Deliverable for Grading

Assignment: Cross-Case Analysis & Peer Review

Submit:

  • A 5-7 minute video presentation of your Case 1 work
  • Written peer reviews (2-3 substantive reviews of classmates' work)
  • Comparative analysis: How did different approaches address similar problems?
  • Reflection on what you learned from peers' approaches
  • Updated research questions or hypotheses for Case 2

Due: End of Week 6

Points: 40
