Week 5: Case 1 Wrap — Your first Case File

Learning Goals

  • Synthesize your work on Case 1 into a coherent case file
  • Document your methodology, findings, and conclusions effectively
  • Reflect on what you learned through the process
  • Communicate technical findings to both technical and non-technical audiences

Activities for the Week

  • Review: Case file template and rubric
  • Workshop: Writing effective technical documentation
  • Peer review: Exchange drafts with classmates for feedback
  • Finalize your Case 1 visualizations and supporting materials
  • Prepare a brief presentation of your key findings

Deliverable for Grading

Assignment: Complete Case 1 File

Submit your comprehensive case file including:

  • Executive summary of your case and findings
  • Detailed methodology section
  • Results with appropriate visualizations and metrics
  • Analysis and interpretation of findings
  • Discussion of limitations and potential improvements
  • Reflection on what you learned
  • Code repository link with documented, reproducible analysis

Due: End of Week 5

Points: 100
