Week 4: Case 1 Deep Dive — Evidence and uncertainty

Learning Goals

  • Evaluate the quality and reliability of evidence in your model's predictions
  • Understand and quantify uncertainty in machine learning outputs
  • Interpret confidence scores, probability distributions, and error metrics
  • Recognize when to trust (or question) your model's conclusions

Activities for the Week

  • Read: "Measuring Confidence and Handling Uncertainty"
  • Watch: "Interpreting Model Outputs" lecture
  • Lab: Running and evaluating your Case 1 model
  • Workshop: Error analysis techniques
  • Discussion forum: Share your uncertainty findings and interpretations

Deliverable for Grading

Assignment: Evidence and Uncertainty Analysis

Submit a detailed analysis that:

  • Presents your model's performance metrics with interpretation
  • Analyzes cases where your model is confident vs. uncertain
  • Documents specific examples of correct and incorrect predictions
  • Discusses what the evidence tells you about your model's strengths and weaknesses
  • Proposes potential improvements based on your uncertainty analysis

Due: End of Week 4

Points: 35
