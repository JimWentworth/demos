Week 10: Cross-Case Checkpoint 2 — Bias, overfitting, false leads

Learning Goals

  • Identify and understand various types of bias in ML systems
  • Recognize signs of overfitting and underfitting
  • Develop critical thinking skills to spot false leads and misleading patterns
  • Learn strategies for mitigating common ML pitfalls

Activities for the Week

  • Read: "Common Pitfalls in Machine Learning"
  • Watch: "Bias and Fairness in ML" lecture series
  • Audit your own Case 1 and Case 2 work for potential biases
  • Group discussion: Sharing examples of bias and overfitting
  • Workshop: Techniques for detecting and preventing overfitting
  • Case study analysis: Famous ML failures and what went wrong

Deliverable for Grading

Assignment: Bias and Pitfalls Audit

Submit a critical analysis that:

  • Audits your Cases 1 and 2 for potential sources of bias
  • Analyzes evidence of overfitting or underfitting in your models
  • Identifies any false leads you followed during your analysis
  • Discusses how these issues might affect real-world deployment
  • Proposes specific mitigation strategies for identified issues
  • Reflects on lessons learned about ML limitations

Due: End of Week 10

Points: 40
