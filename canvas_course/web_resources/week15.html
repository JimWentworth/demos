Week 15: The Reveal — What was actually learned?

Learning Goals

  • Synthesize learning across the entire course
  • Articulate your growth as a data scientist and critical thinker
  • Connect individual skills to broader ML and data science practices
  • Develop a personal framework for approaching new ML challenges

Activities for the Week

  • Final presentations: Share your journey with the class
  • Watch peers' final presentations and provide feedback
  • Reflection workshop: What changed in how you think about ML?
  • Discussion: Future directions and continuing education
  • Course feedback and evaluation
  • Celebrate your accomplishments!

Deliverable for Grading

Assignment: Final Portfolio and Reflection

Submit a comprehensive final package that includes:

  • Final presentation (10-15 minutes) showcasing your best work
  • Complete portfolio with all three polished case files
  • Comprehensive reflection essay (1500-2000 words) addressing:
    • How your understanding of ML evolved throughout the course
    • Key insights and "aha moments" from your work
    • Skills you developed and how you'll apply them
    • Challenges you overcame and what they taught you
    • Your personal framework for approaching ML problems
    • Next steps in your ML learning journey
  • Self-assessment against course learning objectives
  • Professional portfolio materials suitable for sharing with potential employers

Due: End of Week 15

Points: 150
