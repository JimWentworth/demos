Week 14: The Network of Clues — Putting models together

Learning Goals

  • Understand ensemble methods and model combination strategies
  • Learn when and how to combine multiple ML approaches
  • Explore the synergies between different modeling techniques
  • Develop systems thinking about ML pipelines and workflows

Activities for the Week

  • Read: "Ensemble Methods and Model Combination"
  • Watch: "Building ML Pipelines" lecture
  • Lab: Experiment with combining your three case models
  • Discussion: When is model combination beneficial vs. unnecessary?
  • Case study: Real-world ensemble applications
  • Workshop: Designing integrated ML systems

Deliverable for Grading

Assignment: Integrated Model Analysis

Submit a report that:

  • Explores potential combinations of your three case approaches
  • Implements and tests at least one ensemble or pipeline approach
  • Compares integrated performance with individual models
  • Analyzes the trade-offs: complexity vs. performance gain
  • Discusses practical considerations for deployment
  • Proposes an integrated system design for your problem domain
  • Reflects on how different models complement each other

Due: End of Week 14

Points: 45
