Week 8: Case 2 Deep Dive — Interpreting behavior

Learning Goals

  • Develop skills in model interpretation and explainability
  • Understand what your model has learned and how it makes decisions
  • Use interpretation tools appropriate to your chosen method
  • Distinguish between correlation and causation in model behavior

Activities for the Week

  • Read: "Model Interpretability and Explainable AI"
  • Lab: Applying interpretation techniques to your Case 2 model
  • Watch: "Understanding Model Behavior" lecture series
  • Practice: Feature importance analysis and visualization
  • Discussion: What is your model actually learning?

Deliverable for Grading

Assignment: Model Behavior Interpretation Report

Submit a comprehensive report that:

  • Documents your model's decision-making process
  • Analyzes which features are most influential and why
  • Presents multiple interpretation methods and their findings
  • Compares model behavior with your initial hypotheses
  • Discusses unexpected behaviors or surprising patterns
  • Includes clear visualizations of model interpretation

Due: End of Week 8
