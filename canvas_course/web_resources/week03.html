Week 3: Case 1 (Student-Selected Path) — Core Concepts

Learning Goals

  • Select and justify a machine learning approach for your first case study
  • Understand the core concepts underlying your chosen ML method
  • Articulate the assumptions and requirements of your selected approach
  • Begin applying theoretical concepts to practical problems

Activities for the Week

  • Choose your Case 1 path (e.g., classification, regression, clustering)
  • Read: Core concepts chapter for your selected path
  • Watch: Technique-specific video tutorials
  • Exploratory data analysis on your case dataset
  • Peer consultation: Discuss path selection with classmates

Deliverable for Grading

Assignment: Case 1 Path Selection & Initial Analysis

Submit a report that includes:

  • Your selected ML approach and justification for this choice
  • Summary of core concepts and how they apply to your case
  • Initial data exploration findings
  • Preliminary questions and hypotheses about your case
  • Anticipated challenges and how you plan to address them

Due: End of Week 3

Points: 30
